My name is Alex McKnight. I'm a lifelog Oregon resident with a passion for art, media, storytelling. and technology. I've graduated from Portland Community College, and the University of Oregon with a degree in Cinema Studies and Journalism.

I've spent my time with the Daily Emerald's podcast desk as a host reporting on interactive entertainment technology and media, including labor issues and equality within the industry. I've also worked with the University of Oregon's Conference Services to help organize academic conferences and work with clients and exhibitors.

I have a passion for media, including film, games, technology, entertainment as art, as well as the social conditions that affect these mediums and their respective industries.
